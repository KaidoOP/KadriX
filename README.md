# KadriX

**KadriX** is a Bob-built AI campaign flow that turns raw product ideas, text briefs, and demo-video context into launch-ready marketing campaigns faster.

This project was created for the **IBM Bob Dev Day Hackathon** under the challenge theme:

> Turn idea into impact faster

KadriX helps solo builders, startups, and product teams move from an early product idea or app demo to a structured marketing campaign without needing a full creative agency workflow.

---

## Problem

Product teams often already have a working prototype, app demo, or product idea, but turning that raw material into a clear launch campaign takes time.

They need to define:

- What the product does
- Who the target audience is
- What the main value proposition is
- Which campaign angle to use
- Which hooks, ad copies, CTAs, and video scripts fit the product
- How to improve the message after feedback

For small teams, this process can be slow, repetitive, and expensive.

---

## Solution

KadriX provides a simple web-based campaign assistant with a WhatsApp-style chat experience.

Users can submit:

- A short product description
- A campaign goal
- Target audience information
- Desired tone of voice
- Optional demo-video context or transcript

KadriX then generates a launch-ready campaign package including:

- Product summary
- Target audience
- Campaign angle
- Marketing hooks
- Ad copy variants
- Call to action
- Short video ad script
- Improved campaign version based on user feedback
